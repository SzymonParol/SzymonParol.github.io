About Me

I am a Mechatronics Engineer with a Master’s degree in the same field. My interests and professional experience focus on programming microcontrollers and microcomputers, including STM32, ESP32, ATmega, Raspberry Pi Pico, Raspberry Pi Zero, Raspberry Pi 4, and Raspberry Pi 5. Both academically and in personal projects, I have worked on robotics, machine vision, remote control systems, and 3D printing, combining these technologies to develop practical mechatronic solutions. In my professional career, I have worked with industrial automation systems, including solutions developed by Siemens. Currently, I work as a programmer, contributing to applications for database management, web services, graphic generation, documentation parsing and maintenance, as well as video and audio file processing with tools such as FFmpeg. Outside of my technical work, I enjoy sailing on the Masurian lakes, swimming, street workouts, brewing beer, 3D printing, and playing board and computer games. I value quick learning, teamwork, and continuous development, and I am always looking for opportunities to apply my skills in mechatronics, programming, and robotics.
