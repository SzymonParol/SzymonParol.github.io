About Me

I am a dedicated Mechatronics Engineering student currently pursuing my Master’s degree in the same field, with only my thesis defense remaining to complete my studies. My passion lies in programming microcontrollers and microcomputers, including STM32, ESP32, ATmega, Raspberry Pi Pico, Raspberry Pi Zero, Raspberry Pi 4, and Raspberry Pi 5. Both academically and as a hobbyist, I focus on robotics, machine vision, remote control systems, and 3D printing, integrating these technologies to develop innovative mechatronic solutions. In my professional journey, I have worked with industrial automation systems, such as those developed by Siemens. Currently, I am employed as a programmer, where I contribute to the development and maintenance of applications that support database management, web services, graphic generation, documentation parsing and maintenance, as well as video and audio file processing through the integration of tools like FFmpeg. My role involves ensuring the functionality and efficiency of various projects by leveraging my technical skills and problem-solving abilities. Outside of my technical pursuits, I enjoy sailing on the Masurian lakes, swimming, and engaging in street workouts. I also have a passion for brewing my own beer, 3D printing, and playing board and computer games. I pride myself on being a quick learner and a collaborative team player, consistently striving to contribute effectively to any team I am part of. My diverse interests and commitment to continuous learning make me a motivated candidate for opportunities in the fields of mechatronics, programming, and robotics.
